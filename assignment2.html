Schematic

This is my schematic! I chose to use 330 ohm resistors for the RBG LED to be conservative as to not blow out the LED. For red I originally calculated 2V = .02 * R, where R = 100 ohms and green and blue 3.2V = .02 * R, R = 160 ohms, however, when I used 100 and 220 resistors (closest I had to 160), the LED was very bright and hard to look at so I bumped all the resistors up to 300 ohm.
